Does this app support layers? How to create or add layers?

This app supports layers. In fact, we call it objects in this app. When you add a new shape/object/path, some image or some texts, they will be added as a new object. In other words, you can think of the objects as layers. And layers are created and added automatically when you draw, sketch and edit your icon on the canvas. You don't have to create and add new layers manually.
